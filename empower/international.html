Organizations

Save the Children

Save the Children gives children in the United States and around the world what every child deserves -- a healthy start, the opportunity to learn and care when disaster strikes.

The Amala Foundation

The Amala Foundation unites youth from all walks of life and empowers them to grow as individuals and as leaders in service to humanity.

Kiva

Kiva is an international nonprofit with a mission to connect people through lending to alleviate poverty. Kiva celebrates and supports people who are looking to create a better future for themselves, their families and their communities.

UNESCO

UNESCO strives to create holistic policies that are capable of addressing the social, environmental and economic dimensions of sustainable development.
